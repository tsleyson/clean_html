And so, as always, the wind, with an uncritical face,

passes softly between the shy pair.

Will things from a long time ago be forgotten sometime?

Today, we see each other and laugh, and only the gentle heat will remain.

 

Will you end up leaving? Will I go first, I wonder?

But just a little more, I want to be together.

 

Inside my always half-spoken heartbroken

I hold it tightly, this moment.

Even the day I leave on a trip, I practice.

Surely, of course, the common things

are the ones with no substitute. Today things will change.

My memories

are sparkling, and the days of happiness

are radiant.

 

In the transparent future, dreams are piling up.

No one notices the sign that's still small.

Because I've said "I'll be back" so many times,

this is the place that I'll always come back to.

 

I'm always looking for it, wherever it is,

this moment that I want to communicate.

If I want to see you, even though I see you right away,

I remember and feel relieved.

I am considerately with people.

As for my every day

I am not alone, and for being there,

thank you.
