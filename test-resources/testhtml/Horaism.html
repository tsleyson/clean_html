If I could have a wish granted,

it would be for you be always by my side,

 

On the still-long road home,

I hid an unspoken secret in my pocket.

 

I was separated from you,

someone important to me.

Why is it that I just want to forget you? / Why is it that I just want to forget about it?

[double check]

 

Even someone who pays attention to his surroundings

did that, and I ended up alone.

The temporary hope betrayed me,

and look, I got sad and couldn't hear anything.

 

Like that, with the things that surely descend,

I'm always worrying

I laugh, and though I've finished, it's wrong.

If you weren't there,

and I definitely couldn't see you,

that sort of future is awful.

But keep that a secret, okay?

 

On a darkening street, as the lights come on,

so that I won't move on,

I stuff it into my pocket.

 

Saying nothing, I don't move on.

I'm not speaking.

The cold winter wind attacks me, and it hurts.

[The kanji for semeru seem to go with a word that means "criticize" or "blame", but this is a homophone of a word that means "assault", so the word "attack" covers both meanings in English.]

 

Surely, the funny story may become part of the past,

but I halt, even though I understand that.

 

Like that, with the things that surely descend,

I may always be worrying.

I might be noticing, but even so

if the me of tomorrow could definitely fall in love,

it would be okay to have a circuit of positive thoughts, right?

 

Look, I gradually fell in love,

just a little certainly.

At that time, I noticed I was becoming weak.

I'll support you for sure

Because I'll definitely be there.

Facing forward, looking upward, I'll advance!

By my own guidepost.

 

If you weren't here,

and I definitely couldn't see you,

that sort of future is awful.

Stay with me, okay?
