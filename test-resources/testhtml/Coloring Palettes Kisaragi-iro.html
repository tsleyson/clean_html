I just fall down and I'm way too clumsy,

I'm sorry for not being at peace.

But I really love to draw.

My sketchbook immediately fills up.

 

As a substitute for expensive tracing paper,

I use air mail paper.

Even though I'm wearing indoor slippers I notice the grave marker

Once again, I'm sorry.

 

What should I do? I don't use the important things.

They're treasures! A pencil from God.

 

I cut down [danbooru].

I peered out from a picture frame. Art,

the color of the sky, using clouds as a model, is beautiful, isn't it?

 

Because of my aspirations that have just started to move

I expand the palette of colors of my dreams

I dye the painting of the sky and the suneko.

 

When it comes to an accent at lunchtime,

I prefer soy sauce to sauce.

Why don't they have an afternoon class

on the subject of napping?

 

A magic tool shop surrounds me, and I'm happy

I start using a tool for watercolor painting, sky-color

 

A soft-wrapped mug

won't break if I drop it

The warmth even continues softly, for a long time.

 

I go out to buy more unmixed paint with no color [white paint?]

In my sketchbook, there are many dessins, and

I put what I like into all of them.

 

I go past drawing time

In front of my eyes is always the full sky, spreading out.
