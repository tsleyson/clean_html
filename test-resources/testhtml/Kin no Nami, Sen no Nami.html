Kin no Nami, Sen no Nami (Golden Waves, Thousand Waves)

 

My yearning for waves of gold against the shore

on the morning I awaken...

 

When I think strongly of someone

My meeting with that person is held in the palm of my raised hand.

The hope that envelopes this planet is watching the future

that the wind and the direction of the water point to.

 

 

The distant clouds and rays of light sway, as if inviting me in.

My yearning for waves of gold against the shore,

on the morning I awaken,

it begins.

 

The shining rain becomes a gentle song.

I paddle slowly, unafraid.

Whatever time it is, the sky is off with you.

Let me receive the strength of merely looking up at it.

 

It runs up into the heavens as if delivered,

this wish.

And I go towards the distant thousand waves,

further than any dream.

The distant clouds and rays of light sway, as if inviting me in.

My yearning for waves of gold against the shore

awakens.

 

It runs up into the heavens as if delivered,

this wish.

And I go towards the distant thousand waves,

further than any dream.

 

It will begin.

It will begin.
